Publish reports

You’ll need to find out if you need to publish a report.

If you have to publish reports, you need to work out your reporting periods and deadlines. You'll get a customised checklist of the data you’ll need to assemble.

Once you’ve prepared all your report data, you must publish your report. All reports are published online, where anyone will be able to read them.
