How to publsh your report

Complete the following steps

  • When you your report data is ready, it must be signed-off by your board of directors
  • You will need a Companies House log-in that is authorised to publish reports for your company
  • If you don’t have one, you may want to to delegate this task to someone in your organisation who does have one
  • Alternatively you can register your own account on Companies House. The authorisation process involves a paper letter being sent to your Company’s registered address, which can take weeks. Be sure to do this well in advance of your deadline

Once you have followed all of the above guidance, you can return to this website and publish your report.
